Restaurant Website Redesign

I worked on a website redesign project for a local restaurant. The goal was to create a modern and visually appealing website that showcases the restaurant's menu, ambiance, and online reservation system. By incorporating elegant design elements, optimizing the user experience, and ensuring responsiveness across all devices, we were able to enhance the restaurant's online presence and increase customer engagement.

Personal Finance App

I collaborated with a startup to develop a mobile app for managing personal finances. The project involved creating an intuitive user interface, implementing secure data storage, and integrating with various financial APIs. The result was a feature-rich app that empowered users to track expenses, set budgets, and gain insights into their financial health.

E-commerce Website Development

I led the development of an e-commerce website for a fashion brand. The project involved creating a seamless shopping experience, integrating secure payment gateways, and implementing inventory management functionality. The website showcased the brand's products in an elegant and visually appealing manner, attracting customers and driving sales.
